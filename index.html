About Us

About A.V. Foundation

Established in 2018, A.V. Foundation is a premier competitive exam guidance center located in the heart of Kolhapur. We believe in providing a highly supportive environment for all students — whether you are a fresh graduate or a working professional.

Our integrated approach combines expert tutoring with a dedicated, quiet study space to ensure our students achieve their career goals. From MPSC and railway exams to specialized Mathematics and English coaching, we prepare you to compete and succeed.
